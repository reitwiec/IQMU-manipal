Prelims

Held in 2 rounds spread over 2 days, the IQMU preliminary elimination rounds are essentially a battle-royale of quizzers from all over India!

Featured on Day 1 are Classic clinical vignettes waiting to be solved in a race against time. Dubbed as the most crucial step by various testimonials, beware of the clock on this one! Day 2 opens with the second preliminary round and the refreshing view of gross specimens. A potpourri of clinical and para clinical subjects, this round is conquered by shrewd teamwork!

Semi-final

The top 12 teams from prelims shall qualify for the semi-finals and test their wits on the IQMU signature - 'Mental Relay'.

Designed by avid quizzers and subject geeks to keep the whole team on its toes, this round can either make it or break it!

Final

Top 6 teams will then battle it out in a thrilling buzzer round that will surely be akin to walking on a tightrope!

If you’ve got an eye on the prize, get ready to be trigger-happy, because a mind-numbing battle for the top 3 spots ensues.
