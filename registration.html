Rules!

• Each team must comprise of 4 members.
• Maximum of 1 intern allowed. Those who finish their internship before (date) will not be eligible to participate.
• There may be more than one team from a single University (maximum 5 teams).

Indian Teams

• 10% off on total registration fee cost for colleges sending 3 teams.
• 15% off on total registration fee cost for colleges sending 4 or more teams.

Fee Structure

• Registration will be completed after verification of payment.
• Accommodation will have to be paid for separately, the details of which can be availed upon expression of interest in participation.
• Accommodation will be on a first-come-first-serve basis.

National Teams

• Early Bird ₹3200/Team, Late Bird ₹5400/Team.

SAARC Nations

• Early Bird $80USD, Late Bird $100USD.

International Teams

• Early Bird $100USD, Late Bird $120 USD.
